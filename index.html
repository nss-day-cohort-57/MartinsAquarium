Tips and Tricks

Clean your tanks regularly.
Get a feeding schedule.
Do not mix fresh and salt water fish.
Make sure to regulate water temp.
Be sure to have cleaner fish to minimize time spent manually cleaning.
Source quality fish food.
Monitor water PH levels regularly.
